<p><p>The lithium-ion separator market consists of three types: monolayer separator, bilayer separator, and trilayer separator. Monolayer separators are single-layered structures made up of polymer materials and provide basic functionality for battery safety. Bilayer separators have an additional layer, enhancing their thermal stability and increasing battery performance. Trilayer separators have an extra ceramic layer, providing superior thermal stability, higher permeability, and improved overall battery safety. Each type offers different levels of performance and safety features, catering to various battery requirements in the market.</p></p>
