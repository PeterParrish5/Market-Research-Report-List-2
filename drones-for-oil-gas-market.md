<p><p>The drones for the oil and gas market can be classified into three types: fixed-wing, multiple rotor, and hybrid wing. Fixed-wing drones resemble small airplanes as they have fixed wings and require a runway for takeoff and landing. Multiple rotor drones, also known as quadcopters or multirotors, use multiple propellers to fly. They are highly maneuverable and can hover in place. Hybrid wing drones combine features of both fixed-wing and multiple rotor drones, offering the ability to take off and land vertically while also having the extended endurance of fixed-wing drones.</p></p>
