<p><p>Silicone rubber is widely used in the electronic market due to its excellent properties. The market can be classified into two types: Room Temperature Vulcanizing (RTV) and High Temperature Vulcanizing (HTV). RTV silicone rubber is used for various applications where curing at room temperature is preferable. It is flexible, easy to handle, and commonly used for electronic potting, encapsulation, and gasketing. HTV silicone rubber, on the other hand, requires high temperatures for curing. It offers better thermal stability and is suitable for applications requiring high temperature resistance, such as manufacturing electronic connectors and insulating cables.</p></p>
