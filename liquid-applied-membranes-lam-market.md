Elastomeric membranes are synthetic rubber-based and offer high flexibility and durability, making them suitable for applications such as balconies and terraces. Cementitious membranes consist of cement mixed with polymers and are mainly used for waterproofing foundations and concrete structures. Other types of LAM include acrylic-based, polyurethane, and polyurea membranes, each offering unique properties for different applications. These membranes provide a seamless, long-lasting, and reliable solution for waterproofing.</p></p>
