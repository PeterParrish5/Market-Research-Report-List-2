<p><p>The submersible hydrostatic level transmitters market consists of two main types: flanged and threaded. This type is commonly used for larger applications requiring a higher level of stability and accuracy. On the other hand, threaded transmitters have a threaded connection, allowing for a simpler installation process. They are more commonly used in smaller applications where space is limited or where cost is a primary factor.</p></p>
