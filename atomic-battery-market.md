<p><p>The Atomic Battery Market can be divided into two main types: Thermal Conversion Type and Non-Thermal Conversion Type. </p><p>Thermal Conversion Type atomic batteries generate electricity by utilizing heat released from the radioactive decay of isotopes. This heat is then converted into electrical energy through thermoelectric or thermophotovoltaic processes. </p><p>On the other hand, Non-Thermal Conversion Type atomic batteries convert radiation energy directly into electricity. They use materials such as semiconductors or capacitors to capture and transform the radiation energy into electrical power. Both types offer unique advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
