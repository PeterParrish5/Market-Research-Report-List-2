<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Scraped Surface Heat Exchangers (SSHE) are used to efficiently transfer heat between two fluids with varying viscosities. Reciprocating Tubular SSHEs use a reciprocating motion to scrape the heated surface, while rotating tubular SSHEs use a rotating motion. Both types are suitable for high-viscosity fluids. Rotating Plate SSHEs use rotating plates to enhance heat transfer, making them ideal for low-viscosity fluids. These SSHE market types offer different mechanisms for heat transfer, allowing for versatile applications in various industries.</p></p>
