<p><p>Glass Fiber Reinforced Plastic (GFRP) rebars are composite materials consisting of high-strength glass fibers embedded in a polymer resin matrix. These rebars are used in various applications such as bridges, tunnels, marine structures, and buildings.</p><p>The future outlook of the GFRP rebars market is promising. The construction industry is witnessing a shift towards sustainable and eco-friendly materials, and GFRP rebars offer several advantages in this regard. With increasing environmental concerns and stricter regulations on corrosion prevention, the demand for GFRP rebars is expected to rise. Additionally, the rising investments in infrastructure development and urbanization in emerging economies further propel the market growth.</p><p>The current market for GFRP rebars is also favorable. The demand for these rebars is driven by their superior characteristics, such as high strength-to-weight ratio, durability, and resistance to chemicals and rust.
