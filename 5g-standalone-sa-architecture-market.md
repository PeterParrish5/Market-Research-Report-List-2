Femtocell market consists of small, low-power base stations deployed in residences or small businesses to enhance indoor coverage. Pico Cell market involves small-scale base stations deployed in public spaces like shopping malls or stadiums to improve capacity and coverage. Micro Cell market refers to small to medium-sized base stations placed in urban areas for dense coverage and capacity optimization. Lastly, Macro Cell market includes large-scale base stations installed on towers or rooftops to provide wide coverage in urban and rural areas.</p></p>
