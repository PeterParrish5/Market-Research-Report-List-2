<p><p>The Booklet Maker market includes different types of machines based on their level of automation. Manual booklet makers require complete manual intervention throughout the process, from paper alignment to stapling. Semi-automatic booklet makers combine both manual and automated processes, where paper alignment is manual but stapling and folding are automated. Automatic booklet makers are fully automated machines that require minimal human intervention, as they can perform all tasks, including paper alignment, stapling, and folding, automatically. These different types cater to varying needs and preferences of users in terms of convenience and efficiency.</p></p>
