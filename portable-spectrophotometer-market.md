<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The portable spectrophotometer market consists of two main types: single beam type and double beam type. The single beam type spectrophotometer operates by focusing the light on the sample cell and then measuring the transmitted or reflected light intensity. On the other hand, the double beam type spectrophotometer splits the light beam into two paths, one passing through the sample and the other through a reference, allowing for accurate comparison. Both types are widely used for color measurement and analysis across various industries such as pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and food.</p></p>
