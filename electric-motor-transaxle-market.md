<p><p>The electric motor transaxle market is categorized into three types: Wheel Motor Electric Motor Transaxle, Central Motor Electric Motor Transaxle, and In-Wheel Motor Electric Motor Transaxle. </p><p>1. Wheel Motor Electric Motor Transaxle refers to a system in which the motor is directly integrated into the wheel hub, providing power and torque to that specific wheel.</p><p>2. Central Motor Electric Motor Transaxle involves a motor placed in the center of the vehicle, typically connected to the transmission system, distributing power to all wheels.</p><p>3. In-Wheel Motor Electric Motor Transaxle consists of individual motors embedded within each wheel, enabling independent control and power distribution to enhance vehicle performance and efficiency.</p></p>
