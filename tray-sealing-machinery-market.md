<p><p>Tray sealing machinery market consists of three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic machines. Manual machines are operated by hand, typically suitable for small-scale businesses with low production volumes. Semi-automatic machines involve both manual and automated processes, with operators responsible for loading trays and the machine performing the sealing process. Fully automatic machines fully automate the process, from tray loading to sealing, offering high production rates and efficiency. The choice of machinery depends on the business's requirements and production capacity.</p></p>
