<p><p>The Air Booster Compressor market consists of three types: Volumetric, Dynamic, and Thermal. Volumetric compressors work by trapping a fixed volume of air and reducing its volume to increase its pressure. Dynamic compressors, on the other hand, utilize the kinetic energy of air to increase its pressure through centrifugal force. Lastly, Thermal compressors use heat to increase the air pressure, typically by burning fuel. Each of these types has its unique features and applications, catering to different industries' specific needs for compressed air.</p></p>
