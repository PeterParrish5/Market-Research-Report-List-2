<p><p>The Doppler sensor market can be classified into two types: X-Band and K-Band. X-Band Doppler sensors operate at a frequency range of approximately 8 to 12 GHz, offering higher accuracy in detecting and measuring speed, velocity, and distance. On the other hand, K-Band Doppler sensors use a frequency range of about 18 to 27 GHz, providing better performance in terms of detection range and sensitivity for applications such as automotive radar systems. Both X-Band and K-Band Doppler sensors play a crucial role in a wide range of industries including automotive, security, and surveillance.</p></p>
