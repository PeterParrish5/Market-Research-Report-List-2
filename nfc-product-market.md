<p><p>NFC (Near Field Communication) products include NFC controllers and NFC tags. NFC controllers are chips that enable devices to establish communication with nearby NFC-enabled devices. They facilitate contactless transactions, data exchange, and device pairing. On the other hand, NFC tags are small items embedded with an NFC chip that can be attached to objects or places, allowing users to interact with them using their NFC-enabled devices. NFC tags can provide information, trigger specific actions, or facilitate easy wireless connections. Both NFC controllers and NFC tags contribute to the expanding NFC product market.</p></p>
