<p><p>CMP Slurry for Sapphire Wafers is used in the semiconductor industry for the polishing process of sapphire wafers. There are two main types of CMP slurries available: colloidal silica polishing slurry and alumina polishing slurry. Colloidal silica polishing slurry contains tiny silica particles suspended in a liquid, which helps in achieving a smooth and uniform surface finish. Alumina polishing slurry, on the other hand, contains alumina particles that aid in the removal of surface imperfections and scratches, resulting in a polished and defect-free sapphire wafer surface.</p></p>
