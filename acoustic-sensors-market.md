<p><p>Acoustic Sensors are widely used in various industries for different applications. Ultrasonic sensors are one type, which emit and receive sound waves above the human hearing range (typically above 20 kHz). They are used for distance measurement, object detection, and flow measurement. Sound pressure sensors are another type that detect and measure the pressure variations in sound waves. They are commonly used in noise monitoring and environmental applications. Other types of acoustic sensors include piezoelectric sensors, fiber optic sensors, and MEMS-based sensors, which have their specific applications in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
