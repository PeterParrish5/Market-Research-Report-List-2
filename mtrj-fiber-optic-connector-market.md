<p><p>The MTRJ Fiber Optic Connector market is divided into two main types: Singlemode and Multimode. Singlemode connectors are designed for long-distance transmission and use a narrow, tightly focused beam of light. They are commonly used in telecommunication networks. On the other hand, Multimode connectors are used for shorter distances and employ a wider beam of light. They are often employed in data communication systems such as local area networks (LANs). Both types play significant roles in facilitating high-speed, low-loss data transmission in various industries.</p></p>
