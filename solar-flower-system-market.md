<p><p>The Solar Flower System market types can be classified into three categories: On-Grid, Off-Grid, and Others. </p><p>On-Grid systems are connected to the utility grid and generate electricity that can be used directly or sold back to the grid. These systems are ideal for areas with reliable grid connections and net metering policies.</p><p>Off-Grid systems operate independently without any connection to the utility grid. They store surplus electricity in batteries, providing power in remote or rural locations where grid access is limited or unavailable.</p><p>The "Others" category refers to hybrid or decentralized systems that combine the features of both on-grid and off-grid systems. These systems can operate independently while still being connected to the grid when needed, offering flexibility and efficiency.</p></p>
