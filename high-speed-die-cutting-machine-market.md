<p><p>The high-speed die-cutting machine market can be categorized into two types: rotary die cutting machines and platen die cutting machines. Rotary die cutting machines utilize a cylindrical die that rotates to cut materials, making them suitable for large-scale production and quick operation. On the other hand, platen die cutting machines feature a flatbed die that moves up and down to cut materials, making them ideal for smaller-scale production and precise cutting. Both types offer efficient and accurate die cutting capabilities, serving a range of industries such as packaging, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
