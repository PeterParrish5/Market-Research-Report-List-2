<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The N-channel MOSFET transistor market can be classified into two types: enhancement type and depletion type. </p><p>Enhancement type MOSFETs are normally OFF and require a positive voltage to be applied at the gate to allow conduction. They are widely used in digital circuits and power electronics due to their low power consumption and fast switching speed. </p><p>Depletion type MOSFETs, on the other hand, are normally ON and require a negative voltage at the gate to cut off the conduction. They are often used in analog circuits and as a switch in certain applications where the transistor needs to be normally conducting.</p></p>
