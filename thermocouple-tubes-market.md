<p><p>Thermocouple tubes are temperature-sensing devices used to measure temperature in various industrial applications. They consist of two dissimilar metals that are joined together at one end to form a thermoelectric circuit. When there is a temperature difference between the two ends of the tube, an electromotive force is generated, which is measured to determine the temperature.</p><p>The global thermocouple tubes market is expected to grow at a steady pace during the forecast period.
