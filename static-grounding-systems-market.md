<p><p>The Static Grounding Systems market consists of various types of systems that ensure the safe discharge of static electricity to prevent accidents and fires. Universal Static Grounding Systems are designed for general industrial use. Tank Car Static Grounding Systems are specifically meant for grounding railcars during loading or unloading processes. Vehicle-Mounted Static Grounding Systems are installed on trucks and other vehicles to prevent static buildup during transportation. Model C FIBC Container Static Grounding Systems are used for grounding flexible intermediate bulk containers, commonly known as FIBCs, to minimize the risk of electrostatic discharge.</p></p>
