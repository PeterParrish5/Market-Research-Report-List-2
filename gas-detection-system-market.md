<p><p>The gas detection system market consists of various types, including Semiconductor Type, Electrochemical, Catalytic Combustion, Photoionization (PID), and Infrared Sensor. The Semiconductor Type relies on the change in conductivity of a semiconductor material in the presence of a gas. Electrochemical sensors use electrodes to measure the concentration of certain gases. Catalytic combustion sensors detect gases through oxidation reactions. Photoionization (PID) sensors use ultraviolet light to ionize gas molecules for detection. Infrared sensors detect gases based on their absorption of infrared light.</p></p>
