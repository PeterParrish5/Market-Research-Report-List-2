<p><p>Butterfly damper market is categorized into three types: </p><p>1. Manual Type: This type of damper is operated manually by using a hand lever or wheel to open or close the damper. It is cost-effective and suitable for small-scale applications.</p><p>2. Pneumatic Type: These dampers are operated by air pressure. Pneumatic actuators control the opening and closing of the damper, making it suitable for larger systems and requiring less manual effort.</p><p>3. Electric Type: In this type, electric motors control the movement of the damper. It offers greater precision and control, making it ideal for automated systems and applications requiring frequent adjustments.</p></p>
